The Vision

THE BARBERSHOP IS
A CLASSROOM

Jewel Scales' journey began much like the young people this program is designed to serve. Growing up in the community, he understands firsthand the transformative power of a positive mentor.

For over a decade, Scales has honed his craft, observing the barbershop evolve from a place for a haircut into a true community hub. Now, we are using that hub to teach discipline, professionalism, and the confidence that comes with mastering a trade.

THE PROGRAM

A proposed community-based initiative providing at-risk youth with mentorship, life skills, and professional training.

Mentorship

Creating a safe space where young individuals build self-confidence and find positive role models beyond the streets.

Vocational Training

Teaching the art of barbering not just as a job, but as a craft that opens doors to economic independence.

Professionalism

Customer service, financial literacy, and the discipline required to run a successful business.
